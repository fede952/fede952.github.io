Cosmetics giant Estée Lauder notifies customers of a data breach after hackers exploited an Oracle E-Business Suite vulnerability used for HR operations.

{{< cyber-report severity="High" source="BleepingComputer" target="Oracle E-Business Suite HR systems" >}}

Estée Lauder, a global cosmetics company, is notifying customers of a data breach that occurred after attackers exploited a vulnerability in Oracle E-Business Suite. The software was used by the company for human resources operations, suggesting the breach may have exposed sensitive employee or customer data.

The incident highlights the risks associated with third-party enterprise software vulnerabilities, particularly those used for critical business functions like HR.
