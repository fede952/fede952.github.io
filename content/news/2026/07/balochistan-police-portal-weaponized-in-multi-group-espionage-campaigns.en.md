Suspected China- and India-aligned threat actors targeted Pakistani law enforcement agencies from Feb 2024 to Apr 2026, compromising web servers handling police and citizen data.

Cybersecurity researchers have disclosed details of sustained cyber espionage activity against several Pakistani law enforcement organizations, undertaken by suspected China- and India-aligned threat actors between February 2024 and April 2026. The compromised assets included servers hosting web applications that manage police and citizen data, such as criminal records.
