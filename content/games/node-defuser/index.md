The network is infected with **15 hidden virus nodes**. Use logic to reveal safe cells without triggering malware. Numbers tell you how many viruses surround each cell.

{{< game-minesweeper >}}

## How to Play

- **Left click** to reveal a cell
- **Right click** (desktop) or **long press** (mobile) to flag a suspected virus
- Numbers show how many adjacent cells contain viruses
- Reveal all safe cells to defuse the grid
