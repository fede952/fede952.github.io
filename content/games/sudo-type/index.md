Incoming threats detected. The mainframe is under attack. Your only weapon? **Your keyboard.**

Type the falling words before they reach the mainframe. Build combos for bonus points. How long can you defend the system?

## How to Play

- **Type** the falling words to destroy them before they hit the mainframe.
- **Matching** starts automatically — type the first letter of any threat.
- **Combos** multiply your score — chain kills without missing.
- **Speed increases** as your level rises. Stay sharp.
- **Click or tap** the screen on mobile to open the keyboard.
