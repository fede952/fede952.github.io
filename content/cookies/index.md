## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. We also use similar technologies such as `localStorage` (browser storage that never leaves your device).

## 2. What We Use

### Strictly Necessary (no consent required — first-party, never shared)

| Technology | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `localStorage: pref-theme` | Remembers your dark/light theme choice | Until you clear it |
| `localStorage: menu-scroll-position` | Restores the menu scroll position | Until you clear it |
| `sessionStorage: adblock_acknowledged` | Remembers that you dismissed the ad-blocker notice | Browser session |
| Service Worker cache | Speeds up repeat visits (offline support) | Managed by browser |

### Analytics (only with your consent)

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics 4 | Aggregate usage statistics | Up to 14 months |

Our second analytics tool, **Umami, uses no cookies at all** — it is completely cookieless and anonymous.

### Advertising (only with your consent)

Google AdSense and its certified partners may set cookies (e.g. from `doubleclick.net`) to show and measure ads, and — with consent — to personalize them. Details: [How Google uses cookies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies).

## 3. Managing Your Consent

- **Cookie Settings** — use the link in the footer of every page to reopen the consent banner and change or withdraw your choices at any time.
- **Browser controls** — you can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
- **Google controls** — [adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com) (ad personalization) and the [GA opt-out add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- **Industry opt-out** — [youronlinechoices.eu](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu/).

## 4. One Important Note

The interactive tools on this site process your data **100% locally in your browser** — they set no tracking cookies and upload nothing. See the [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy/) for details.
